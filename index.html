Feed Your Mind

Mind is going to learn new habits, ideas, beliefs, no matter what happens, however, You should be the one deciding what and how it learns.

You become what you feed your mind. The ideas or opinions you surround yourself with on a day to day basis are ultimately the ideas or opinions that morph your actions. Before an action there is a thought whether it be conscious or subconscious.

Stop Eating Bad Thoughts

Hear me out, because this is important, the more you repeat a lie the more you will be believe it is true. The more you attention you give to negative thoughts the more your actions and life work in making does thoughts reality.

Avoid Negative Ideas,
Opinions, Emotions

Take a moment and read the following quote:

When you change your thoughts and perception, you change the way you live. -Rita Zahara
Negative thoughts, opinions, ideas only seek to reinforce the belief that a situation is bad, or that we have no control, or we're not doing enough being enough. It's Bullshit negative thoughts are unnecessary. It's unhealthy for your mind to eat negativity on a day to day! Situations can be challenging or uncomfortable, however, it shouldn't be perceived as bad or negative, but as an opportunity for positivity.

Feed Your Mind The Right Thoughts

Good Actions Come From Good Thoughts. Your mind doesn't take action without you thinking first.

Whether it is conscious or unconsciously your positive, encouraging, and inspired thoughts will push your mind to take the corresponding actions.
